  • Monthly credit: $65 onsite service credit added each billing cycle while enrolled in Hybrid. Applied to onsite labor only.

Plans cover day‑to‑day troubleshooting, configuration, updates, and user support. Projects (new device rollouts, migrations, major redesigns, data recovery, cabling, etc.) are quoted separately at project rates.
